# 🚀 Invoice Genome Agent - Feature Breakdown

This document covers our custom engineering layers designed to elevate the standard touchless processing agent into a production-ready enterprise copilot.

---

## 🔥 Our Custom Enhancements

### 1. Transparency & Accuracy Layers
* **Reliability Meter:** Calculates a real-time confidence score for every extracted element before processing.
* **AI Explanation Window:** Breaks down exactly why a document was processed a certain way or why an anomaly was flagged.
* **Invoice Integrity Score:** Scores vendor reliability and histories to preemptively catch fraudulent patterns.

### 2. Intelligent Automation & Safety
* **AI Manager Copilot + Voice AI:** Integrates voice command protocols for hands-free manager overrides and audit approvals.
* **Policy Guardian:** Checks every timesheet against internal corporate compliance criteria automatically.
* **Smart Correction AI:** Contextually auto-corrects structural, typo, or minor formatting issues on the fly.

### 3. Insights & Communication
* **Trend Analysis & Finance Insight Hub:** Aggregates billing metrics over time to expose organizational spending trends.
* **Resolution Box + One-Way Email:** A dedicated review deck coupled with automated system email alerts to notify vendors of resolution flags.
