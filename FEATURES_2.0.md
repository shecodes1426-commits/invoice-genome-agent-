# 🚀 Invoice Genome Agent - Complete Feature Specification 2.0

This document tracks the comprehensive breakdown of all features, baseline workflows, and premium enhancements engineered for our enterprise-grade Touchless Invoice Copilot.

---

## ⚙️ 1. Baseline Functional Requirements
These form the core processing architecture of the invoice pipeline:

* **Multi-Format Input Support:** Seamless capturing capabilities for diverse incoming data streams, including PDFs, raw images, emails, and handwritten notes.
* **AI-Powered OCR & Data Extraction:** Context-aware text and field extraction utilizing advanced vision-language models and prompt engineering.
* **Human-in-the-Loop Exception Handling:** Automated structural and confidence guardrails that seamlessly route low-confidence extractions to human operators for manual verification.
* **Invoice Tracking & Approval Dashboard:** A unified administrator control panel to monitor invoice life cycles, audit extraction details, and issue final sign-offs.
* **Automatic Invoice Generation:** Dynamic generation of compliant, clean corporate digital invoices directly from validated system data fields.

---

## 🔥 2. Premium Custom Enhancements
Advanced operational and security layers designed to scale the agent into a robust corporate financial copilot:

### 📊 Transparency & Risk Identification Layers
* **Reliability Meter:** Calculates a real-time, granular confidence score for every individual data point before finalizing extraction.
* **AI Explanation Window:** Breaks down internal system and model logic to explain exactly *why* a document was processed a certain way or why an anomaly was flagged.
* **Invoice Integrity Score:** Evaluates historic tracking parameters to analyze systemic billing errors or structural document flaws.

### 🧠 Intelligent Automation, Negotiation & Safety
* **AI Manager Copilot + Voice AI:** Integrates smart voice command structures for conversational search requests, hands-free manager overrides, and rapid executive audit reviews.
* **Policy Guardian:** Automatically inspects and cross-references every timesheet or incoming billing statement against localized corporate criteria and internal compliance compliance rules.
* **Smart Correction AI:** Contextually and autonomously auto-corrects minor structural inconsistencies, obvious typos, or minor formatting mismatches on the fly.


### 🏦 Financial Insights & Operational Comm Hub
* **Role-Based Access Control & Auto-Categorization:** Custom portal entry points tailored for distinct system roles (Vendors, Managers, Accounts Payable) with automated routing of incoming data.
* **Trend Analysis & Finance Insight Hub:** Aggregates multi-month enterprise spending and billing metrics over time to expose organizational trends and insights.
* **Resolution Box + One-Way Email:** A dedicated review queue paired with an automated outbound system alert engine to notify external vendors of resolution flags.
* **Automated Reminder Engine:** Proactive system rules that handle automated schedule tracking and auto-send alert notifications for pending or delayed actions.
